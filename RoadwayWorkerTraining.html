Who needs it?

"Any employee of a railroad, or of a contractor to a railroad, whose duties include inspection, construction, maintenance or repair of railroad track, bridges, roadway, signal and communication systems, electric traction systems and roadway facilities who work on or near the railroad track, or who have the potential to foul the railroad track." (FRA Section 214.7)

What about the course?

Our course comprehensively covers 49 CFR 214 Roadway Worker Protection training requirements. The self-guided interactive CD-Rom training program will ensure that you are in compliance with the FRA regulation.

How do you stay certified?

Since the law requires that you take the course every calendar year, we have made it easy for you to re-train. If you have taken our CD-Rom training previously, you are eligible for our new condensed re-training program. This quicker paced program can be completed in a few hours thus ensuring you remain a certified Roadway Worker. The re-training program is offered at the reduced cost of $75.00 US (S&H extra).
